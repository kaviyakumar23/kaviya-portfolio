Is it worth paying for? If you're coding regularly, absolutely. The time you save on debugging, refactoring, and writing boilerplate code pays for itself. Not to mention the stress you avoid by having an expert assistant always ready to help.

If you're on the fence about trying the pro version, give it a shot. Your future self will thank you for the hours saved and headaches avoided.
